Welcome, Donor!

Age: Most blood donation centers require donors to be 18 or older, with some allowing 16-year-olds with parental consent.

Weight: A minimum weight of 45 kg (around 110 pounds) is generally required.

Health: You should be in good general health, feeling well, and not experiencing any acute illnesses.

Hemoglobin: Hemoglobin levels should be at least 12.5 g/dL.

Blood Pressure, Pulse, and Temperature: These should be within normal limits for the individual's age and health.
